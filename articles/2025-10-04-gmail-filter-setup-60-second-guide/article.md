\## How to Create and Organize Email Filters in Gmail
Here's your practical win for today. I'll walk you through setting up a Gmail filter for the First AI Movers newsletter—and you can adapt this for any other subscription or email.
![](<https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/dd7fbff7-2581-4009-9ff1-908c00b1bbbc/d209d3b165d64d348de207095cd2a9c3-386052ba5903e3e3-full-play.gif?t=1759577049>)
Link to Loom: [<https://loom.com/share/d209d3b165d64d348de207095cd2a9c3>](<https://loom.com/share/d209d3b165d64d348de207095cd2a9c3?utm_source=www.firstaimovers.com&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=60-second-gmail-pro-auto-organize-your-inbox-2025-guide>)
\*\*Step 1: Access Your Email\*\* \[0:00]\(<https://loom.com/share/d209d3b165d64d348de207095cd2a9c3?t=0&utm_source=www.firstaimovers.com&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=60-second-gmail-pro-auto-organize-your-inbox-2025-guide>)
![](<https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/4ae39217-e363-48df-ac72-e7630dc69805/11ceccd523fb436bad5a871993e897bb.jpg?t=1759576640>)
\*   Open your Gmail account.
\*   Locate the email address you want to filter. 
\*\*Step 2: Create a Filter\*\* \[0:23]\(<https://loom.com/share/d209d3b165d64d348de207095cd2a9c3?t=23&utm_source=www.firstaimovers.com&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=60-second-gmail-pro-auto-organize-your-inbox-2025-guide>)
![](<https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/b027d4ed-f7c2-424c-9ae5-62299f0e23f6/ea150417204446708d42c42ffcbbb54e.jpg?t=1759576640>)
\*   Click on the email address.
\*   In the search bar, type 'from:' followed by the email address, e.g., \*\*newsletter@firstaimovers.com\*\*.
\*   Click on 'Create filter'.
\*\*Step 3: Choose Filter Actions\*\* \[0:37]\(<https://loom.com/share/d209d3b165d64d348de207095cd2a9c3?t=37&utm_source=www.firstaimovers.com&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=60-second-gmail-pro-auto-organize-your-inbox-2025-guide>)
![](<https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/c52335c0-e37f-49d7-b27c-eac9264abbf8/62d6f569425d4e8f82248032a78ee34a.jpg?t=1759576639>)
\*   Decide what you want to do with the filtered emails:
    \*   Skip the inbox (archive it).
    \*   Apply a specific label (e.g., 'Newsletter').
    \*   Create subfolders for better organization.
\*\*Step 4: Additional Filter Options\*\* \[0:57]\(<https://loom.com/share/d209d3b165d64d348de207095cd2a9c3?t=57&utm_source=www.firstaimovers.com&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=60-second-gmail-pro-auto-organize-your-inbox-2025-guide>)
![](<https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/3deb9329-d82c-4fe0-acc6-6a4dc2e1518a/aac04662eda14a98afca152f37a52c26.jpg?t=1759576640>)
\*   You can also:
    \*   Automatically archive emails.
    \*   Forward emails to another address (for advanced users).
    \*   Ensure emails never go to spam.
\*\*Step 5: Mark as Important\*\* \[1:07]\(<https://loom.com/share/d209d3b165d64d348de207095cd2a9c3?t=67&utm_source=www.firstaimovers.com&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=60-second-gmail-pro-auto-organize-your-inbox-2025-guide>)
![](<https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/03536c15-c68b-4b2c-976c-027d52ce3c81/88005a8a6f6341729dffc46a7a98e7e9.jpg?t=1759576640>)
\*   Always mark important emails to ensure they are delivered to your inbox.
\*\*Step 6: Use Categories\*\* \[1:17]\(<https://loom.com/share/d209d3b165d64d348de207095cd2a9c3?t=77&utm_source=www.firstaimovers.com&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=60-second-gmail-pro-auto-organize-your-inbox-2025-guide>)
![](<https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/b6a8ea94-a3c3-40ce-94d9-ec6f834266b6/c2d4e97dbff24788a8331c4a41acb911.jpg?t=1759576640>)
\*   If you prefer not to use labels or folders, consider using Gmail's categories.
\*\*Step 7: Apply Filter to Existing Emails\*\* \[1:40]\(<https://loom.com/share/d209d3b165d64d348de207095cd2a9c3?t=100&utm_source=www.firstaimovers.com&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=60-second-gmail-pro-auto-organize-your-inbox-2025-guide>)
![](<https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/0e9a01bf-3d2b-4fa1-b2d3-b28a1c873d4c/acbb78895d694784b3b6fcfad5c18473.jpg?t=1759576640>)
\*   The filter will also categorize and classify all past emails that match the criteria.
\*\*Step 8: Review and Finalize\*\* \[1:40]\(<https://loom.com/share/d209d3b165d64d348de207095cd2a9c3?t=100&utm_source=www.firstaimovers.com&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=60-second-gmail-pro-auto-organize-your-inbox-2025-guide>)
![](<https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/32c21154-3d4b-4f05-be55-33e650dff50e/acbb78895d694784b3b6fcfad5c18473.jpg?t=1759576640>)
\*   Review your filter settings and finalize to organize your email effectively.
