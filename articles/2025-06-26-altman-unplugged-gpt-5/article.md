### Strategic Takeaways

1.  **Timeline compression:** If GPT-5 lands within weeks, your competitive moat is iteration speed, not parameter count.
2.  **Talent economics:** Nine-figure packages show scarcity; cultivate in-house upskilling and share upside via liquidity events.
3.  **Agent era:** Build for delegation (tool calling, long-running jobs) rather than pure Q&A; APIs like MCP and Mariner are your friend.
4.  **Global chessboard:** Regulatory headwinds could bifurcate models—keep a China-compliant plan if you serve APAC.

## Fun Fact — OpenAI’s $1 B Pledge Was Mostly IOUs

When OpenAI launched in 2015, the founders announced **$1 B in pledged donations**, but public filings show only about **$130 M** actually arrived before the 2019 pivot to a capped-profit LP. The gap sparked the hybrid for-profit structure we see today.
