# About Dr. Hernani Costa

Dr. Hernani Costa is the founder of [First AI Movers](https://firstaimovers.com) and [Core Ventures](https://coreventures.xyz), and co-founder of [Tarucca](https://tarucca.com) and [Desapega](https://desapega.nl).

**Credentials:** PhD in Computational Linguistics (University of Malaga), MSc and BSc from the University of Coimbra. 25+ years in technology, AI systems, and research.

**Focus areas:** AI strategy, EU AI Act compliance, AI governance, fractional CTO services, AI literacy training, responsible AI implementation.

**This repository** contains the canonical text archive of all First AI Movers articles, published under [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/). All articles represent original research and analysis by Dr. Hernani Costa.
