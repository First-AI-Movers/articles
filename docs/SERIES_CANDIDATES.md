# Series Candidates — Editorial Discovery Report

Generated: 2026-04-29

## Methodology

Discovery used four signals:

1. **Explicit markers** — titles containing `(Part N/M)`, `(Day N/M)`, `#N`, "Episode", or "Chapter".
2. **Repeated title prefixes** — three or more articles sharing the first 3–4 words.
3. **Thematic clusters** — articles covering the same framework, city pattern, or technology family.
4. **Canonical URL patterns** — Radar serials or multi-part Insights posts.

A *series* requires intentional ordering and narrative progression. A *topic cluster* is multiple articles on the same subject without a prescribed reading order. This report distinguishes both.
